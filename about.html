About ChippyCraft

Where engineering meets art — ChippyCraft creates one-of-a-kind modern-industrial furniture pieces that bring light, metal, and epoxy together in ways you’ve never seen before.

Our Story

ChippyCraft began with a simple idea: metal and light could be more than functional — they could be expressive. What started as a small welding project quickly evolved into a design journey, where every table and chair is a statement piece, built by hand with the precision of an engineer and the creativity of an artist.

Each piece we make is meant to feel alive — glowing from within, reflecting its surroundings, and transforming ordinary spaces into immersive environments.

Our Craft

Every ChippyCraft creation blends industrial durability with aesthetic imagination. From infinity-lit tables to weather-resistant outdoor designs, our work is guided by balance — strength and elegance, structure and light.

We don’t mass-produce — every weld, every epoxy pour, every reflection of light is intentional. Built to last. Designed to impress. Crafted to be yours.
